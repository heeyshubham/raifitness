Phase 2 — Content System

Four content types.
Each one does a specific job.

🏗️
Credibility Content
Factory floor reels, weld quality close-ups, manufacturing process. Answers: "Can I trust this brand?"
"Every machine starts here — our Vasai factory floor."
📚
Education Content
Gym setup guides, equipment checklists, buyer tips. Gets saved & shared — highest algorithmic signal.
"5 things no one tells you before buying commercial gym equipment."
⭐
Social Proof Content
Client gym walkthroughs, before/after installs, testimonial reels. Removes hesitation in warm leads.
"150 machines. Pune. Setup in 21 days."
📩
Lead Generation Content
Keyword CTAs, catalogue drops, WhatsApp hooks. Designed not for likes — but for DMs and enquiries.
"DM 'SETUP' to get our free 2026 Gym Planning Guide."
Phase 3 — Video Strategy

3 scripts per month.
Each one is a sales tool in disguise.

01
The Factory Floor Reel
Raw, real, 30–45 sec. Manufacturing process footage with voiceover. Builds trust that competitors who just resell imported equipment can never replicate. Target reach: 50K–200K views per Reel once algorithm momentum builds.
02
The Buyer Education Reel
45–60 sec. Addresses one question a gym entrepreneur is actively Googling. Positions Rai Fitness as the expert, not just the vendor. High save rate = algorithm push to non-followers.
03
The Client Gym Showcase
30–45 sec walkthrough of a real installed gym with stats — city, machine count, timeline. The most powerful conversion content because it answers: "Have they done this before? Can they do it for me?"
